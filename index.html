Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number (Target) at the beginning of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific (static) amount of points to your total score.


You will win the game by matching your total score to the Target number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the Target number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it (the value of an additional click remains constant at that same value).


Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of the crystals and display 4 new crystals.
